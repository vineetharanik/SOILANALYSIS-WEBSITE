🌿 Urea - Detailed Information

Urea Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Urea is a nitrogen-based fertilizer with 46% nitrogen content. It's a highly concentrated fertilizer.

💧 Application: Urea can be applied in the soil during sowing or during the growing season as top dressing. It dissolves quickly in the soil.

🌱 Benefits: Urea promotes vigorous leafy growth and enhances plant metabolism, helping the crop utilize sunlight and other nutrients efficiently.

⚠️ Caution: Overuse can lead to nitrogen burn in plants, so it's important to follow recommended dosages and timings.

🌾 Crops for Urea: Ideal for cereals, vegetables, and fruits that require significant nitrogen for leaf growth.
