🌿 Ammonium Sulphate - Detailed Information

Ammonium Sulphate Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Ammonium Sulphate contains 21% nitrogen and 24% sulfur.

💧 Application: It is best applied in the soil before planting or during the growing season as a side dressing to enhance plant growth.

🌱 Benefits: It provides nitrogen for vegetative growth and sulfur for better protein synthesis and plant health.

⚠️ Caution: Overuse may lead to excessive nitrogen levels and soil acidification.

🌾 Crops for Ammonium Sulphate: Ideal for crops like corn, soybeans, and other legumes that thrive in slightly acidic soils.
