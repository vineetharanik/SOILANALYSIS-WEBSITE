🌱 Soybean - Detailed Crop Information

📍 Soil Type: Soybean grows well in black soil with good drainage and moderate fertility. Loamy soils are also ideal.

☀️ Climate: Soybean requires moderate temperature, ideally between 20°C to 30°C. It needs plenty of sunlight.

💧 Water Requirements: Soybean requires moderate water, but overwatering can lead to root rot. Proper drainage is essential.

📅 Growing Season: Soybean is grown during the Kharif season. Sowing is done in June–July, and harvesting happens after 4–5 months, typically in November–December.

⏳ Time to Harvest: Soybean matures in about 120 to 150 days.

🐛 Common Pests & Diseases: Soybean is susceptible to pests like aphids and whiteflies, as well as diseases like rust and blight.

💊 Fertilizer Suggestions: Use balanced NPK fertilizers in a ratio of 20:60:40. Avoid overuse of nitrogen-based fertilizers.

🌾 Average Yield: Average yield is 12–18 quintals per hectare.

✅ Tips for Better Growth:

  • Use disease-resistant seeds to prevent crop loss.
  • Proper weed control helps soybean growth during the early stages.
  • Provide adequate irrigation during dry periods.
  • Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion.
