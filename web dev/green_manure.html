🌿 Green Manure - Detailed Information

Green Manure Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Green manure is made from plants grown specifically for soil improvement, such as legumes, clover, and mustard.

💧 Application: Green manure plants are grown and then incorporated into the soil before they flower or mature.

🌱 Benefits: Improves soil fertility, adds organic matter, fixes nitrogen, and helps prevent soil erosion.

⚠️ Caution: Use at the right time before planting crops to ensure effective decomposition.

🌾 Crops for Green Manure: Suitable for all crops, particularly those grown in organic farming systems.
