🌿 Bone Meal - Detailed Information

Bone Meal Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Bone meal is made from finely ground animal bones and is rich in phosphorus and calcium.

💧 Application: Bone meal can be incorporated into the soil during planting or applied as a top dressing.

🌱 Benefits: Promotes strong root development, boosts flowering, and improves fruiting.

⚠️ Caution: Overuse can cause phosphorus imbalances in soil.

🌾 Crops for Bone Meal: Best suited for flowering and fruiting crops like tomatoes, peppers, and root vegetables.
