🌿 Single Super Phosphate (SSP) - Detailed Information

SSP Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: SSP is a phosphorus-based fertilizer that contains 16-20% phosphorus and 10-12% sulfur.

💧 Application: SSP can be applied to the soil before sowing or as a side dressing for better root growth.

🌱 Benefits: Enhances root development, flowering, and overall plant health.

⚠️ Caution: Avoid applying too much as excessive phosphorus can harm plant growth.

🌾 Crops for SSP: Ideal for root crops, legumes, and flowering plants.
