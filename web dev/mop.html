🌿 MOP (Muriate of Potash) - Detailed Information

MOP Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: MOP is a potassium-based fertilizer that contains 60% potassium chloride (KCl).

💧 Application: MOP is commonly used as a top dressing or incorporated into the soil during planting for enhanced fruiting and resistance to diseases.

🌱 Benefits: Enhances fruiting, improves resistance to disease, drought tolerance, and overall plant strength.

⚠️ Caution: Over-application can lead to salt accumulation in the soil, affecting plant growth.

🌾 Crops for MOP: Ideal for crops that require potassium, such as fruits, vegetables, and flowering plants.
