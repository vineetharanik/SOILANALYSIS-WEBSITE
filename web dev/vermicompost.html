🌿 Vermicompost - Detailed Information

Vermicompost Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Vermicompost is made from organic waste broken down by earthworms, resulting in nutrient-rich compost.

💧 Application: It can be added to the soil as a top dressing or mixed in the soil during planting. It can also be used to improve soil health.

🌱 Benefits: Improves soil structure, retains moisture, encourages beneficial microorganisms, and boosts plant growth.

⚠️ Caution: Overuse may lead to excess organic material in the soil, affecting other nutrients.

🌾 Crops for Vermicompost: Suitable for all crops, especially organic farming and garden plants.
