🌿 Compost - Detailed Information

Compost Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: Organic fertilizer made from decomposed organic matter, including plant and animal waste.

💧 Application: Compost is applied directly to the soil, enriching it with nutrients over time. It can be spread before planting or used as a top dressing during the growing season.

🌱 Benefits: Improves soil structure, promotes beneficial microorganisms, increases soil fertility, and retains moisture.

⚠️ Caution: Ensure compost is well-decomposed to avoid introducing pathogens into the soil.

🌾 Crops for Compost: Ideal for all crops, especially for organic farming practices.
