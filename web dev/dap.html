🌿 DAP (Diammonium Phosphate) - Detailed Information

DAP Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: DAP is a phosphorus-based fertilizer with high phosphorus content. It also contains nitrogen.

💧 Application: DAP is typically applied to the soil during sowing or early growth stages for strong root development.

🌱 Benefits: Enhances root development, improves flowering and fruiting, and promotes overall healthy plant growth.

⚠️ Caution: Avoid excessive application as it may lead to imbalances in soil nutrients.

🌾 Crops for DAP: Ideal for crops that require strong root growth like vegetables, legumes, and fruit crops.
