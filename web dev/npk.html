🌿 NPK (Commercial Mix) - Detailed Information

NPK Fertilizer

🔬 Composition: NPK is a balanced fertilizer containing nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) in varying proportions.

💧 Application: It is applied based on crop needs, generally during sowing or early growth stages.

🌱 Benefits: Supports balanced plant growth, encourages flowering, fruiting, and root development.

⚠️ Caution: Over-application can lead to soil nutrient imbalance and water pollution.

🌾 Crops for NPK: Suitable for all crops, especially when balanced nutrition is required.
