🌱 Sunflower - Detailed Crop Information

📍 Soil Type: Sunflower grows best in black soil with good drainage. Loamy and sandy soils are also suitable.

☀️ Climate: Sunflowers require a warm climate, with an ideal temperature range between 20°C to 30°C. They need full sunlight for proper growth.

💧 Water Requirements: Sunflowers need moderate watering, but they are tolerant to dry conditions. Overwatering should be avoided.

📅 Growing Season: Sunflowers are generally grown during the Kharif season, sowing occurs between June and July, and harvesting is done in 4–6 months.

⏳ Time to Harvest: Sunflowers typically mature in about 120–150 days.

🐛 Common Pests & Diseases: Sunflowers may be affected by pests like aphids, sunflower beetles, and rust.

💊 Fertilizer Suggestions: Apply balanced fertilizers with NPK ratio 50:50:50. Sunflowers benefit from organic manure to improve soil quality.

🌾 Average Yield: Average yield is around 12–18 quintals per hectare with proper management.

✅ Tips for Better Growth:

  • Plant sunflower seeds at a proper depth to ensure good germination.
  • Ensure adequate space between plants for proper air circulation.
  • Control weeds early in the growing season to avoid competition.
  • Use organic fertilizers to promote sustainable farming.
